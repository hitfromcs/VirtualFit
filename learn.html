Welcome to Virtual Fit

Revolutionizing online clothing shopping with 3D try-on technology and intelligent outfit recommendations.

What is Virtual Fit?

Virtual Fit is an innovative e-commerce solution that allows users to try on clothes virtually using 3D models tailored to their body metrics. It brings personalization and accuracy to online shopping like never before.

Why Virtual Fit Matters

One of the biggest challenges in fashion e-commerce is the high rate of returns due to size, color, or fit issues. Virtual Fit reduces return rates significantly by letting customers visualize clothing on a model that matches their body shape, size, and skin tone—leading to better purchase confidence.

Technological Innovation

This platform integrates PHP-based backend logic, user data processing (like height, chest, waist, skin tone), and a 3D engine using Three.js to load `.glb` models dynamically. Our solution merges server-side intelligence with immersive frontend visuals.

Best Color Contrast Outfit Algorithm

Our system intelligently recommends the best contrasting outfit combinations for users. For example, selecting a white shirt will automatically pair with black pants, ensuring visual harmony and style. The logic adapts based on color psychology and fashion contrast principles.

Interactive 3D Preview

Users can view a real-time 3D model wearing their selected clothing combination. Based on their body metrics and preferences, the model dynamically updates to reflect personalized outfit choices—plus 4 recommended looks for contrast inspiration.
